5-Hit Game for Toyama
No matter what the Ozaukee Nuggets threw at him today, Yuusaku Toyama hit it. He easily handled Nuggets pitching, going 5 for 6 and helping the Cowboys to a 11-1 triumph at Nuggets Ballpark.

"The way I was swinging today, I think I could have closed my eyes and hit the ball," he told BNN reporters in the postgame interview.

Told of Toyama's quip, Nuggets skipper Mike Angiolillo said he "would take him up on that next time."

Yuusaku Toyama singled in the 1st, hit a two-run single in the 2nd, grounded out in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off Tse-tung Ow in the 6th, singled in the 7th and hit a solo-shot off Greg Jones in the 9th.

Toyama is batting at a .299 pace this season, picking up 26 hits in 87 at-bats with 6 home runs, 14 RBIs and 9 runs scored.
