Condors Prevail, 3-1, Over Florence

Josh Higgins brought the hometown crowd to its feet with no one out in the bottom of the fourth. That's when the 2048 Most Valuable Player Award winner belted a solo home run. With the round-tripper, the Condors took the lead, 1-0.

"Win or lose, we pick each other up in this clubhouse," Pacheco told the Menomonee Today.
