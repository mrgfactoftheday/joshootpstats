St. Croix Nabs 1-0 Lead With Thrilling Win\n
The St. Croix Blue Sox started the Divisional Round in dramatic fashion today, stealing Game 1 from the Eau Claire Seafarers in a walk-off in the 11th inning. St. Croix took the 2-1 win thanks in large part to a good day from starter Craig DeHart. The left-hander threw 6 innings of shutout ball in the win, which gave the Blue Sox a 1-0 lead to start the best-of-3 series.

The clutch hit was provided by Chase Carlisle. He delivered a walk-off run-scoring single off Jim Lennon in the bottom of the 11th.

"Both teams played hard," said St. Croix manager Luis Soto.

Game 2 is set to be played tomorrow at Blue Sox Ballpark. Slated to start are Rich Lamb for the Eau Claire Seafarers and Francisco Galba for the the St. Croix Blue Sox.
