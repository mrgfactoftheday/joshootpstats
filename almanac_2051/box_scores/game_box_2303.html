Nuno Belts Bunch of Base Hits
There aren't many who could be pro ballplayers and there are even fewer who could put up 5 hits in a day.

"We made some bad pitches and he took advantage of it," said Crawdads manager Chris Rieck. "That's the game, though. Don't make mistakes and you'll win ballgames. Make mistakes and, well, you saw what happened today."

Daniel Nuno singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, doubled in the 6th, doubled in the 7th and hit an RBI double in the 9th.

For the season Nuno has produced 18 hits in 56 at-bats for a .321 average with no home runs and gotten on base at a .333 clip.
