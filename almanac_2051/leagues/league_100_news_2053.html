Prospects Clinch Wild Card
Tuesday, August 30th, 2050
It wasn't quick or easy for the Washburn Prospects, but they have clinched a berth in the postseason.

While they couldn't capture the Northern Counties District 7 West crown, the club has cinched up a wild card berth late in the season.

"You come close to not getting a chance in the postseason and it starts to worry you," said closer Luis Cortes. "This is a huge relief for our guys and we're going to celebrate it."

The team will be looking for its 1st Wisconsin Baseball League championship.
