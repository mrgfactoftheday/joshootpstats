Seafarers Advance to Divisional Round
Monday, September 5th, 2050
With a 6-2 win, the Eau Claire Seafarers are off to the Divisional Round.

They ousted the Rusk Firebirds at Eau Claire Fields tonight in the one-game Wildcard Series format.

Eau Claire was led by second baseman Butch Kane, who went 1-for-4 to pace his team to victory.

"If it wasn't me stepping up, it would have been someone else, that's just the way our team has been this year," he said. "We like our chances in the Divisional Round. We're a dangerous team right now."

The Divisional Round matchups and schedule will be announced after the Wildcard Series finishes.
