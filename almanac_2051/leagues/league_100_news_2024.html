Northern Counties District 7 East Champs Crowned: Price Penguins
Thursday, August 25th, 2050
The Price Penguins have won the Northern Counties District 7 East.

"We made it. Everything we talked about doing in spring training has come true so far," said Oliver Partlow. "We're not done though, there's still a larger goal in mind."

Price has a record of 59-34 record and will by vying for their 1st Championship. It was the club's 2nd Northern Counties District 7 East title.

"You never get sick of celebrating something like this," said first baseman Dave Huerta.
