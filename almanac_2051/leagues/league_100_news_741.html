Droney Twirls Seafarers to Shutout Victory
Monday, July 11th, 2050
Mike Droney of the Eau Claire Seafarers was cool and collected on the mound, leading his club to a shutout victory over the Iron River Sharks, 2-0.

"He was mixing his pitches real well and keeping his composure on the mound," Eau Claire manager Greg Harrison said later. "When Mike does that, good things happen."

The right-hander had 7 strikeouts, no walks and allowed 3 hits in the win.

In 11 starts this season Droney has rung up a 4-2 record and a 0.89 ERA.
