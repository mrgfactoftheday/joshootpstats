SL1 Reliever of the Year Award Winner Named
Wednesday, August 10th, 2050
2050 was a year to remember for Jason Rice.

The Lake Geneva Marlins reliever was the owner of a 1.02 ERA this season over 44 innings of work. He made 31 relief appearances and had 3 saves, 77 strikeouts, 16 walks and a record of 1 win and 1 loss.

He's also now got some hardware, as the 28-year-old has been named the Sub League 1 Reliever of the Year Award winner.

"It's something I can tell my grandchildren some day, pretty special," he said.

He received 72 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Tyler Hunter of the Wind Lake Fire Fighters finished second in voting, while David Osio of the Merton Enforcers finished third.
