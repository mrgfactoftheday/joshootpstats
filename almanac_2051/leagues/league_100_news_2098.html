Ironmen on to Divisional Round
Sunday, September 4th, 2050
The Crawford Ironmen stepped up when it counted and are off to the Divisional Round.

With a 2-0 win at Seminoles Ballpark, Crawford has eliminated the Milwaukee Seminoles and won the Wildcard Series.

"Our guys knew what it took to win and we stepped up when we most needed to," said starting pitcher Sterling O'Connor. "We need that same intensity and effort in the next round too."

O'Connor was named series MVP as he threw 7 innings, with 8 strikeouts, while allowing 3 hits, no walks and no runs.

Seminoles manager Bill Bruckman said his team is already focused on getting back to the playoffs and having a better result next year.

The Divisional Round matchups and schedule will be announced after the Wildcard Series finishes.
