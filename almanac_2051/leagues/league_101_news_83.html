Morales Sets Down 27 in a Row
Saturday, April 16th, 2050
With the final, flawless out recorded, the Altoona Stone Crabs piled onto Bobby Morales at the center of the Ballpark of Mineral Point diamond. Morales joined baseball's most hallowed club today, completing a perfect game against the Mineral Point Magicians.

The 26-year-old right-hander kept the Mineral Point offense guessing all day long and with some help from the defenders behind him, etched his name into the record books with the 2-0 shutout.

"His breaking pitches had more movement than I've ever seen from Bobby," Altoona manager Izzy Castillo said in the postgame interview. "He made everyone's job easy today. He just saw a target and he hit it. Simple as that."

For the game, Morales retired all 27 batters he faced and fanned 6 of them. He walked none and nobody got on base.
