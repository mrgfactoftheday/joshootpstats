Blue Sox Prevail, as League Finals Goes to the Wire
Sunday, September 25th, 2050
The St. Croix Blue Sox are off to the Championship after an exciting League Finals triumph.

St. Croix downed the Price Penguins, but needed the entire series to do so, with a 5-1 win tonight at Penguins Ballpark wrapping up the series 4-3.

"It's every guy in this room that made this happen. Without us all pulling in the same direction, we don't win this series," said Chase Carlisle, who earned MVP honors for a standout offensive series.

Carlisle put up a .250 with a .379 on-base percentage in the series. He also had 2 home runs, drove in 5 runs and scored 3 runs himself.

The Championship matchups and schedule will be announced after the League Finals finishes.
