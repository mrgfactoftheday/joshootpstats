Meinert on Fire: 5-for-5 for Ashland
Sunday, May 22nd, 2050
There aren't many who could be pro ballplayers and there are even fewer who could put up 5 hits in a day.

Mark Meinert is one of those rare few, as he went 5-for-5 against the Sawyer Steelers at Steelers Stadium today. The monster effort from the 25-year-old center fielder helped the Ashland Aztecs take the win, 4-3.

"We made some bad pitches and he took advantage of it," said Steelers manager Vince Schenk. "That's the game, though. Don't make mistakes and you'll win ballgames. Make mistakes and, well, you saw what happened today."

Mark Meinert singled in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Mark Gernaat in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, doubled in the 8th and hit an RBI double in the 9th.

For the season Meinert is hitting .412, batted in 7 runs with 2 home runs and 5 runs scored.
