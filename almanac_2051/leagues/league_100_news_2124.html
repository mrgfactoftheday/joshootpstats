Ravens Prevail, as League Semi-Finals Goes to the Wire
Thursday, September 15th, 2050
Iowa Ravens won a nail-biting series against the Wallworth Thunder Cats 3-2, wrapping up the League Semi-Finals finale 7-3 at Wallworth Fields.

Ravens first baseman Jarrod Hughes was named the series MVP.

He batted .444, drove in 3 runs and scored 4 to lead Iowa into the next round of play.

"Jarrod was real solid for us, real solid," said Ravens manager Kevin Vespucci. "I normally say I'd like to have nine of him out there, but thankfully we got by with just one this time."

The League Finals matchups and schedule will be announced after the League Semi-Finals finishes..
