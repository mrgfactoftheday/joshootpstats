Spiders Ross Gets No-Hitter
Wednesday, July 13th, 2050
Excitement reigned at Condors Stadium today as Taylor Spiders hurler Josh Ross tossed a baseball rarity -- a no-hitter.

After defeating the Menomonee Condors, 2-0, he was swamped by reporters in the clubhouse for postgame comments.

"I'm just glad to be here," Ross said. "Today I just went right at the Condors as hard as I could, as long as I could."

Ross chalked up 3 strikeouts and allowed 1 walk in his superb performance.

"I don't think they could have stopped him with a howitzer today," said Taylor manager Craig Foley.

So far this year Ross has 6 wins and 3 losses and a 2.41 ERA.
