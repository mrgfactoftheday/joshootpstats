Rock RP Hastings Will Miss 4-5 Weeks
Thursday, July 14th, 2050
Rock Swingers reliever Travis Hastings left yesterday's game against the Grant Express after suffering a strained shoulder throwing a pitch. Hastings was taken to the clubhouse for examination by team doctors. A team spokesman told BNN today Hastings is expected to miss 4-5 weeks.

This season Hastings has claimed no saves and registered a 1-4 record with a 4.17 ERA. He has 8 relief appearances spanning 41 innings and has struck out 15.
