Nunez wows fans with the long ball
Monday, May 30th, 2050
The 2050 Home Run Challenge provided a raucous crowd at Penguins Ballpark with great drama. Outlasting the competition was Mike Nunez. The Price Penguins third baseman knocked off Josh Higgins in the final round by a score of 13-12.

"What a unique experience," said Nunez. "It's something I'll always remember."

In 757 career games Nunez is hitting at a .216 pace with 576 hits with 91 doubles, 36 triples and 87 homers while scoring 308 runs and racking up 328 RBIs. He logged 343 walks and owns a .317 on-base percentage.
