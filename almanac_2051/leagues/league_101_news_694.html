Mt. Horeb Narrowly Wins Sub League 1 Division 3
Saturday, July 2nd, 2050
Mt. Horeb Swordsmen manager Elijah Queen said it was a "battle all season long" for his club to win the Sub League 1 Division 3. But they've done exactly that, today edging out their division rivals to take the top spot.

"It was down to the wire, so it makes it even more special," he said. "Our guys grinded all year and this is the end result."

The club has a 47-33 record to top the division for the 7th time and will now be searching for a 1st championship.

"We may not have won the division by a wide margin, but I don't think anyone in our clubhouse ever doubted we'd do it," said reliever Victor Hernandez.
