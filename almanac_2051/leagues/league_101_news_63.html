Johnson Spins No-Hitter
Sunday, April 10th, 2050
Ladysmith is abuzz in the wake of a masterful no-hitter by Greg Johnson.

The 27-year-old hurler was dominating against the Evergreen Wild Things, leading the Scots to a 4-0 win at Scots Ballpark today.

"I could tell early on that it was going to be a long day for us," Evergreen manager Marty Carey said afterward. "He made us look real bad. He was that good. And still going strong at the end of the game."

Johnson had 12 strikeouts and gave up 4 walks en route to the no-hitter.

"Yeah, this was a special day for me," Johnson said. "I had my good stuff and good movement. Good location, too. Even when I made a mistake, I got away with it today."
