Playoff Bound: Railhawks Win Wild Card
Saturday, July 2nd, 2050
The North Fond du Lac Railhawks are celebrating a trip to the postseason and say they plan to make the most of the opportunity.

The team officially clinched a wild card berth today and starting pitcher Jose Carrero said they are "looking forward to making some noise" in the playoffs.

"There's nothing better than postseason baseball," he said. "We hope to go on a deep run and give our fans in North Fond du Lac something to be excited about."

For now, excitement is running high as the team's 44-36 record has clinched the playoff position and the team has a chance to win its 1st WBL Triple A championship.
