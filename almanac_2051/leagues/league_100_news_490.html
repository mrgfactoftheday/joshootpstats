Sunday, June 12th, 2050
"It's worse than we thought." That was the news from the Barbarians clubhouse today as it was learned that catcher Edgar Padilla will be out for an added one week as a result of a recent injury. "We'd rather take this thing slowly and make sure Edgar gets back onto the field healthy," said the team's head trainer.

Looking at his present stats, Padilla has compiled a .184 batting average in 23 games with a .244 on-base percentage, while hitting 1 home run and driving in 3 runs.
