Yes, It's a No-No by MacDougall
Thursday, April 28th, 2050
Art MacDougall relied on excellent pitch selection and pinpoint control to hold Lake Mills hitless in a 4-0 win at Ballpark of Hales Corners today.

"Nobody goes out to pitch a no-hitter," he said later. "We just go out and try to get a win. A 10-9 win is better than a 1-0 loss. My pitching buddies might not be too happy giving up nine runs but a win is a win. The key to it today was I mixed my pitches well. I kept the White Knights off balance and guessing the whole game."

The 28-year-old starter rung up 5 strikeouts and 2 walks in whipping the White Knights.

"When a guy is throwing strikes the way he was and is locating well, it is going to be a long day for any club," Lake Mills skipper Brian Schatzer said.

In 5 starts this season MacDougall has rung up a 3-2 record and a 1.49 ERA.
