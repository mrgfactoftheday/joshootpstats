Barry Pitches Gem against Calumet
Wednesday, August 24th, 2050
The Dunn Raccoons did little to support starter Lyndon Barry against the Calumet Surf, but it turned out he didn't need the help.

The right-hander spun a complete game shutout against the Surf, striking out 3 and surrendering 4 hits. The performance from Barry allowed Dunn to escape with the 2-0 win despite an anemic offense.

"Nobody came to me and said 'Lyndon, we're gonna need you to win this one for us today,' but I could feel it," Barry told BNN reporters following the win. "I'm the guy that will step up for you when you need it."

Barry has gone 4-7 this season with an ERA of 1.69 and 71 strikeouts in 106.2 innings pitched. Other clubs are hitting .148 against him.
