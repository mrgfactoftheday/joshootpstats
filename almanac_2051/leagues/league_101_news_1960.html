2050 SL1 Pitcher of the Year Award Named
Saturday, August 13th, 2050
The Wind Lake Fire Fighters have a rising superstar or maybe even a budding Hall-of-Famer in the making.
It's Mario Guerrero, this year's Sub League 1 Pitcher of the Year Award selection. This blossoming hurler wowed fans, peers and sportswriters with a fabulous performance.

Guerrero compiled a 6-1 record in 15 starts, tallied 94 strikeouts in 103 innings and recorded a 0.52 ERA. Other clubs managed only a .116 batting average against him.

He received 63 first place votes out of a possible 72. Jason McDonnell of the Prescott Waves finished second in voting, while Mike Singleton of the Viroqua Red Claws finished third with 9 first place votes.
