Richardson Spins Masterpiece, No-Hits Waushara
Tuesday, May 31st, 2050
In a dominant outing, Dave Richardson of the Jackson Cowboys totally silenced the bats of the Waushara Sharks, finishing with a no-hitter.

In the Cowboys' 5-0 victory, Richardson faced only 31 batters, surrendered no hits, had 6 strikeouts and 3 walks.

"Honestly, I didn't even know the situation was what it was until the seventh inning," Jackson manager Troy Harris said. "I happened to look up there to see how many hits we had gotten, and I happened to notice that they hadn't gotten any."

Commenting on the defeat, losing manager John Gould told reporters in the clubhouse that he had a "pretty simple" analysis of the game.

"The Cowboys had all the hits and all the runs, so they won," he snapped. "But yeah, Richardson pitched a heck of a ballgame."
