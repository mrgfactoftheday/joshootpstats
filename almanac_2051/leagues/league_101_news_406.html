Ochoa No-Hits Waveriders
Friday, June 3rd, 2050
Luck is abuzz in the wake of a masterful no-hitter by Jorge Ochoa.

The 20-year-old hurler was dominating against the Waterloo Waveriders, leading the Warriors to a 1-0 win at Waveriders Ballpark today.

"I could tell early on that it was going to be a long day for us," Waterloo manager Tony Hernandez said afterward. "He made us look real bad. He was that good. And still going strong at the end of the game."

Ochoa had 4 strikeouts and gave up no walks en route to the no-hitter.

"Yeah, this was a special day for me," Ochoa said. "I had my good stuff and good movement. Good location, too. Even when I made a mistake, I got away with it today."

This season Ochoa has gone 2-7 with an ERA of 3.37, posting 35 strikeouts in 66.2 innings.
