O'Connor Earns SC's Best Pitcher Honor
Thursday, September 1st, 2050
Sterling O'Connor's been around the league long enough to appreciate the honor he received today. After going 4-1 in August, the 33-year-old Crawford hurler has been named Southern Counties Pitcher of the Month.
