Woodford Announces Retirement
Monday, July 18th, 2050
Russ Woodford said he's incredibly grateful for getting a chance to play with the Cross Plains Northmen organization, but this season will be his last.

"I'm extremely loyal to the team, but that's why I have to walk away. I think there's probably someone younger and better who can come in and take my spot," the 36-year-old said. "I'm grateful for all the opportunities I've gotten in pro ball."

A check of his lifetime stats shows Woodford has played in 429 games with a .161 batting average. Among his 215 hits are 65 home runs. He also has scored 158 runs and driven in 190 runs.
