Playoff Bound: Fighting Scots Win Wild Card
Wednesday, August 31st, 2050
Cheers, laughter, and even a few tears filled the Dodge Fighting Scots clubhouse as the team celebrated a Southern Counties wild card berth.

"We didn't win the division like we wanted to, but I'm still smiling," said first baseman Reece Foss. "In the end, we've still got a chance to win the Championship and that's the important thing."

The club has a 54-44 record and will be seeking the 1st Wisconsin Baseball League championship in team history.
