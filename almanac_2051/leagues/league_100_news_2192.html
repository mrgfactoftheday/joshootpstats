Bisbing Wins Northern Counties Pitching Crown
Thursday, October 20th, 2050
An old hand in this game, Chris Bisbing of the Marinette Millionaires silenced critics as well as he silenced the opposition this year by winning the Northern Counties Pitcher of the Year Award.

The 35-year-old starter threw 151.1 innings, notched 142 strikeouts and put up a 11-2 record to claim the coveted award.

He received 70 first place votes out of a possible 72. Tyler Bradley of the Douglas Black Bears finished second in voting with 2 first place votes, while Mike Droney of the Eau Claire Seafarers finished third.
