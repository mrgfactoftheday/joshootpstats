Saavedra Is SC's Top-Rated Rookie
Tuesday, October 18th, 2050
Today the Southern Counties announced the winner of the 2050 Rookie of the Year Award. The top-ranked rookie was the talented Wallworth first baseman Marcos Saavedra.

The 36-year-old Thunder Cats newcomer took the trophy by hitting .216 in 79 games this season and finished with 56 hits, 13 home runs, 39 RBIs and 31 runs scored.

He received 69 first place votes out of a possible 72. Jim Orr of the Ozaukee Nuggets finished second in voting, while Jonathan Conrad of the Vernon Browns finished third with 3 first place votes.
