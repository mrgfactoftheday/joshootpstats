Enforcers Celebrate Wild Card Win
Friday, July 1st, 2050
It wasn't quick or easy for the Merton Enforcers, but they have clinched a berth in the postseason.

While they couldn't capture the Sub League 1 Division 2 crown, the club has cinched up a wild card berth late in the season.

"You come close to not getting a chance in the postseason and it starts to worry you," said reliever Eddie Gonzalez. "This is a huge relief for our guys and we're going to celebrate it."

The team will be looking for its 1st WBL Triple A championship.
