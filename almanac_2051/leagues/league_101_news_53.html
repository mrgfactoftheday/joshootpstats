Magicians Strmiska Silences Bloomer
Friday, April 8th, 2050
The Mineral Point Magicians shut out the Bloomer Revolution 5-0 today behind the strong pitching of Kevin Strmiska. Strmiska surrendered only 2 hits in recording the complete-game victory, and struck out 6.

Following the last out, Strmiska appeared to celebrate a little too boisterously for Bloomer manager Jason Castle, who could be seen scolding the famously outspoken right-hander as the clubs left the field. Strmiska's own manager then intervened with some words for his counterpart.

The Revolution skipper would not comment on the exchange, but at the Magicians press conference, Strmiska confirmed that it was regarding the celebration.

"He basically told me I was being a lousy winner," Strmiska said.

"And I told him he was being a lousy loser," Mineral Point manager Seth Elswick added.
