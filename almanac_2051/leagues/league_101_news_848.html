Recovery Slow For River Bandits Joe Graham
Friday, January 13th, 2051
Things have gotten worse for Joe Graham of the Berlin River Bandits. The 34-year-old starting pitcher will not be back as soon as originally expected. Joe Graham is expected to undergo surgery in Berlin(Wisconsin) tomorrow.
