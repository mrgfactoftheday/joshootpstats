de Reus Twirls No-Hitter Against Lake Wisconsin
Friday, June 3rd, 2050
It was a fantastic day for Coleman Airhogs starter Jasper de Reus and a lousy day for the Lake Wisconsin Green Wave.

The Coleman hurler's pitching artistry completely befuddled and bewildered the Green Wave, as the Airhogs cruised to a 2-0 win. When it was all over, de Reus was credited with one of the rarer feats in baseball -- a no-hitter.

"The ball was moving and darting, and best of all, going where I wanted it to go," de Reus said later.

He rang up 5 strikeouts and allowed 4 walks while shutting Lake Wisconsin down at Ballpark of Coleman.

So far this season de Reus has a 4-2 record, 2 starts, 39.2 innings, 27 strikeouts and a 3.18 ERA.
