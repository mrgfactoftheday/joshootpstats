Will Takuzo Yoshizawa be elected to the Wisconsin Baseball League Hall of Fame?
Wednesday, December 28th, 2050
While it's not a sure thing, four-time All-Star Takuzo Yoshizawa is expected to have the votes to go into the Wisconsin Baseball League Hall of Fame for 2051.

The annual class will be announced next week, and Yoshizawa is expected to receive the highest percentage of votes for his outstanding 19-year career.

Having retired in 2045, he said he planned to go into the Hall as a member of the Eau Claire Seafarers.

Voters are reminded that ballots may be cast up until the votes are announced on January 4th, 2051.

Reviewing his career stats, Yoshizawa has worked 2083 innings with 1764 strikeouts en route to a 127-85 record. The 44-year-old also has a 1.70 ERA.
