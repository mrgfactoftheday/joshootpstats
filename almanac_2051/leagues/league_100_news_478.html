Seaton Says Goodbye in Polk
Saturday, June 11th, 2050
Dave Seaton said he's incredibly grateful for getting a chance to play with the Polk Blue Jays organization, but this season will be his last.

"I'm extremely loyal to the team, but that's why I have to walk away. I think there's probably someone younger and better who can come in and take my spot," the 39-year-old said. "I'm grateful for all the opportunities I've gotten in pro ball."

Seaton has played in 786 games and is hitting .233 lifetime with 189 RBIs, 331 hits, 69 doubles, 5 triples and 56 home runs. He has scored 176 times and stolen 4 bases.
