SC Batting Title Goes to Monroe's Garcia
Saturday, September 3rd, 2050
Many talented Wisconsin Baseball League players can go their entire careers and never even get a sniff of a batting title.

Melvin Garcia has already captured one at age 24.

The Monroe shortstop batted .293 to capture the 2050 Southern Counties batting championship.

He collected 110 hits, 33 doubles, 9 triples and no home runs for the year. In 100 games Garcia totaled 41 RBIs and scored 41 runs.
