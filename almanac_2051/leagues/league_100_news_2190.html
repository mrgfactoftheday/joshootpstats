Rich's Treatment Not Going Well, Suffers Setback
Wednesday, October 19th, 2050
Kewaunee Chargers reliever Frazer Rich got another setback today. His doctors informed him that his injury was not responding to treatment as well as they hoped and that he would need more time to rehabilitate. If all goes well, they expect Rich to be ready to go within 3-4 weeks. He told BNN, "I didn't expect a strained hamstring to take this long to heal."
