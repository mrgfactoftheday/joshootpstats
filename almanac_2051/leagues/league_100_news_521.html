Douglas Black Bears Owner Brad Meeks Passes Away
Wednesday, June 15th, 2050
It was learned today that Brad Meeks, the owner of Douglas Black Bears, had passed away. No cause of death was announced. The elder Meeks has been suffering health problems for the last few years and had already turned over much of the team operation to his son, Brad Meeks Jr.. Speaking for the family, the Douglas general manager said that Brad would assume the club presidency and handle day-to-day operations. The younger Meeks Jr. is reputed to be economizer in fiscal matters and tolerant in personnel relationships.
