Total Control: Cross Plains No-Hit by Arriaga
Monday, June 27th, 2050
It was a terrific day for Alex Arriaga of the Kimberly Raiders at Raiders Ballpark today. The left-hander stood out with a fantastic 3-0 no-hitter against the Cross Plains Northmen.

"I knew I had a no-no going in about the sixth inning when I noticed no one was talking to me anymore," he said, referring to the time-honored superstition of avoiding the pitcher so as not to jinx the no-hitter. "Don't let people fool you. Everybody on the field and in the ballpark knows what's happening. They just pretend they don't."

For the game, Arriaga totaled 2 walks and 9 strikeouts in a very strong outing.

"His mechanics were perfect, and I don't think he threw one bad pitch in the whole game," Kimberly manager Jeremy Berg said. "He was that good."

This year Arriaga has fanned 65 in 66.2 innings and collected a 5-1 record with a 1.21 ERA.
