Red-Hot McDowell Cools Off Kewaskum
Sunday, May 22nd, 2050
The Mt. Horeb Swordsmen did little to support starter Jonathan McDowell against the Kewaskum Islanders, but it turned out he didn't need the help.

The left-hander spun a complete game shutout against the Islanders, striking out 9 and surrendering 2 hits. The performance from McDowell allowed Mt. Horeb to escape with the 1-0 win despite an anemic offense.

"Nobody came to me and said 'Jonathan, we're gonna need you to win this one for us today,' but I could feel it," McDowell told BNN reporters following the win. "I'm the guy that will step up for you when you need it."

Thus far this season, McDowell has worked 49.1 innings with 41 strikeouts en route to a 5-1 record and a 1.09 ERA.
