Thrilling Win Pushes Taylor to Next Round
Monday, September 5th, 2050
Josh Ross said he was extremely nervous before taking the mound in the Wildcard Series.

"How could you not be?" Ross said. "There's not much margin for error in a one-game series and that's nerve-wracking."

But the Taylor hurler soon settled in, and it showed. His club came out on top, with a 2-1 win at Ballpark of Taylor over the Burnett Spikes, to advance to the Divisional Round.

"Once you get going, you sort of calm down a bit," Ross explained. "But we're still relieved to get the win, that's for sure."

Ross was named MVP after throwing 5 innings, with 2 strikeouts, while allowing 3 hits, 3 walks and 1 run.

Burnett manager Nick Moles said his club was "disappointed, but not devastated" and he promised they would be back contending for another Championship next season.

The Divisional Round matchups and schedule will be announced after the Wildcard Series finishes.
