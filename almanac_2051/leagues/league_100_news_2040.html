Milwaukee Clinches Wild Card
Monday, August 29th, 2050
The "ultimate goal" remains in reach for the Geoff Bailon and the Milwaukee Seminoles.

The Southern Counties District 1,4,5 squad today captured a wild card berth and Bailon said the team's plan is simple.

"Get to the playoffs, win the Championship," he said. "If that's not your goal as a Wisconsin Baseball League team, I don't know what's wrong with you."

If the Seminoles were to win the Championship, it would be the 1st championship trophy in franchise history.
