Orcas Crowned Kings of the Sub League 2 Division 2
Monday, June 27th, 2050
Fans of the Ripon Orcas have a lot to get excited about, as the club has captured the Sub League 2 Division 2 crown.

It's the 7th time in franchise history that the club has finished atop the standings and they now have an opportunity to capture the franchise's 1st WBL Triple A championship.

Manager Venceslau Gralhez said there's "a lot of excitement" in Ripon over the club right now and they hope to live up to expectations.

For his part, reliever Reginaldo Savio said the division championship is "just a start" for his team.

"We want to go all the way, win the World Series," he said. "Winning the division - we could get used to this."
