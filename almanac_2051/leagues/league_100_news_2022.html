La Crosee Wins Southern Counties District 3 South Crown
Thursday, August 25th, 2050
The La Crosee Argonauts are champions of the Southern Counties District 3 South, but don't plan the parade quite yet.

"It's a nice accomplishment, but we haven't done anything until we win the Championship," said starting pitcher Matt Hunt. "We're going to celebrate this for sure, but the real celebration is still to come for fans in La Crosee."

The club's record currently sits at 57-36. It is the team's 5th division title and they will be seeking the franchise's 1st Wisconsin Baseball League championship.

Team manager Travis Beu said he'll take some time to celebrate the division title, but then it's "back to work" for members of the team and coaching staff.
