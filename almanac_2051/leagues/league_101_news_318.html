Erick Salas out 7-8 Weeks With Injury
Tuesday, May 24th, 2050
Baseball can be a contact sport at times. It can exact a harsh toll on a player's body. In a recent game against Peshtigo Turtles, Wild Things starting pitcher Erick Salas was injured throwing a pitch and is expected to miss 7-8 weeks with a rotator cuff strain. The team did not announce any roster moves today related to this injury. The Evergreen skipper told BNN that personnel decisions would be announced as early as tomorrow.

Season-to-date Salas has rung up 3 wins and 3 losses in 9 appearances. He has an ERA of 0.87 and has fanned 34 batters in 51.2 innings.
