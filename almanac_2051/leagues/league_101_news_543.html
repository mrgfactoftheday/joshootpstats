Enforcers Baca Suffers Setback
Sunday, June 19th, 2050
Just when it looked like Tony Baca would soon return to the Merton Enforcers, the reliever suffered yet another setback in his recovery from a strained shoulder. According to his doctors, treatment was progressing well until yesterday, when Baca experienced quite a bit of pain during a workout. Further examination revealed he had caused more damage and would now be disabled for at least 2 weeks.

To date in the season, Baca is 3-0 with 25 relief appearances and no saves. He has held opposing batters to a .092 average, pitched 31.1 innings, struck out 19 and walked 18 while recording a 0.29 ERA.
