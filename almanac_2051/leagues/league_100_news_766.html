Player of the Week: Gutierrez of the Invaders
Monday, July 18th, 2050
Antonio Gutierrez is respected by most Oconto Invaders fans for his no-frills style of play. The 27-year-old first baseman went about his business with vigor last week to grab the Northern Counties Player of the Week honors.

He compiled a .407 average (11-for-27) with 5 home runs, 8 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

For the year Gutierrez is batting .299 with 8 home runs.
