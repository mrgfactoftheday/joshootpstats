Blockbuster Trade! Vasquez for Garcia
Sunday, April 3rd, 2050
The Monroe Wizards made a major move this afternoon, shipping 31-year-old RHP Jonathan Vasquez and 23-year-old minor league RHP Roberto Murillo to the Douglas Black Bears for 27-year-old C Luis Garcia. Wizards general manager Chris Roesel explained the trade, saying "To add a player of this ability, you've got to give up a good player."

"That's just how it works," he said.


For his career Vasquez has picked up 51 wins and 30 losses with 13 saves, a 1.71 ERA and 837 strikeouts in 871.1 innings.

Garcia has a career .176 batting average and .259 on-base percentage. He has recorded 69 hits with 14 doubles, 1 triple, 2 homers, 34 RBIs and scored 20 runs since coming into the league.
