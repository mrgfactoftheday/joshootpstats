Singleton Sends Jackson Bats to Deep Freeze
Tuesday, June 7th, 2050
Mike Singleton kept the opposition off the scoreboard and the Viroqua Red Claws were all smiles after winning 5-0 against the Jackson Mules at Red Claws Ballpark today. The left-handed Singleton gave up no runs in his start, permitted 2 hits and had 6 strikeouts.

"If there was a stat for 'team shutout,' this would be it," Singleton said later. "My catcher called a great game, guys delivered at the plate and when I messed something up the guys behind me were where they needed to be."

To date Singleton is 5-4 in 11 starts with a 1.38 ERA and has 67 strikeouts in 78 innings.
