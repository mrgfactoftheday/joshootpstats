Richland Goes Hitless Against Hsia
Tuesday, July 12th, 2050
Lafayatte Dingos starter Fei-hsien Hsia came up big today at Cannonballers Ballpark with an exceptional 2-0 outing against the Richland Cannonballers. Not only did he whip them, but Hsia held them hitless, too.

"My arm was a bit sore in warmups," confessed Hsia. "Sometimes you go out there and you're not feeling all that hot. You get into the game and all of a sudden in the seventh inning you look up and see the scoreboard and think to yourself, 'Oh, my goodness, I have a no-hitter!' You're so focused on what you're doing, it just happens."

In the course of the no-hitter, Hsia collected 7 strikeouts and surrendered 1 walk -- a day good enough to get his name in the history books.

To date this season Hsia is 6-2 with 11 starts and an ERA of 1.07 with 78 strikeouts in 76 innings.
