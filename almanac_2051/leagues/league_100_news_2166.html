Marty Garrett Buys the Forest Bobcats
Friday, October 7th, 2050
The Forest press and public got a chance to meet and greet the new owner of the Forest Bobcats today at Bobcats Ballpark.

The new head man is Marty Garrett, a well-known business tycoon. He bought the club from Danny Schilhab in a straight cash sale a week ago.

The WBL commissioner announced completion of the deal and approval by a vote of the owners earlier this week.

Garrett has acquired a ball club that ended up with a 43-57 record last season and was 10th in the Northern Counties District 7 East standings.

Garrett has a reputation for being lenient in disposition and relating to others and for being controlling in financial dealings.
