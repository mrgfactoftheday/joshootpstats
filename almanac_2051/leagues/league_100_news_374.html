Castillo Humbles St. Croix
Tuesday, May 31st, 2050
The Wood Revolution shut out the St. Croix Blue Sox 6-0 today behind the strong pitching of Jesus Castillo. Castillo surrendered only 3 hits in recording the complete-game victory, and struck out 7.

Following the last out, Castillo appeared to celebrate a little too boisterously for St. Croix manager Luis Soto, who could be seen scolding the famously outspoken right-hander as the clubs left the field. Castillo's own manager then intervened with some words for his counterpart.

The Blue Sox skipper would not comment on the exchange, but at the Revolution press conference, Castillo confirmed that it was regarding the celebration.

"He basically told me I was being a lousy winner," Castillo said.

"And I told him he was being a lousy loser," Wood manager Jason Peterson added.

This season Castillo has compiled a 1-0 record with a 0.00 ERA.
