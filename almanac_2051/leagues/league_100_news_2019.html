Esquivel Shuts Out Barracudas
Wednesday, August 24th, 2050
In an outstanding mound performance, Marathon Prairie Thunder starter Raul Esquivel fired a complete-game shutout against the Portage Barracudas at Barracudas Ballpark today.

Esquivel was the key to the Prairie Thunder's 5-0 win. The 32-year-old left-hander was in high spirits on the mount for much of the game, his mood largely undented by the 2 hits and no walks he allowed.

"2 hits?" he said with a laugh following the game. "Listen, I'd be happy with a 30-hitter as long as it was a shutout."

This season to date Esquivel has a record of 5 wins and 5 losses with a 1.02 ERA, has appeared in 19 games and struck out 135 in 124 innings.
