Week's Top Performer Honored by SC
Monday, July 11th, 2050
Marcos Saavedra, known affectionately as "the old man" by the younger players in the Thunder Cats clubhouse, showed his teammates how to do it last week -- and in the process the 36-year-old vet beat out all competition for the Southern Counties Player of the Week Award.

He put up a .412 batting average, 7 hits in 17 at-bats, 3 home runs and 6 RBIs. Saavedra also scored 6 runs.

Thus far in 2050 Saavedra is hitting .255 with 7 home runs, 16 RBIs, 18 runs scored and has compiled a .430 OBP.
