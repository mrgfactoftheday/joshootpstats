Southern Counties District 1,4,5 Champions Crowned
Wednesday, August 24th, 2050
The manager of the Wallworth Thunder Cats said "a lot of hard work, talent and luck" are the reasons for his team's Southern Counties District 1,4,5 championship.

"This club believes in themselves and goes out and gets it done," said Troy Lieberman. "You can't ask for much more than that as a manager."

It's the club's 6th division crown and gives them an opportunity to win the franchise's 1st Championship.

"We want that title badly," said reliever Ben Jack as he took a break from celebrating with teammates. "This is just a start."
