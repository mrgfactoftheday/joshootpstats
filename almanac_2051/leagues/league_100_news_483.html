Gonzalez Fashions Shutout Against Seafarers
Saturday, June 11th, 2050
In an impressive outing, Alejandro Gonzalez whitewashed the Eau Claire Seafarers 7-0.

"I'm getting a lot more downward movement instead of side to side. That side-to-side stuff can get hit a long way," said Gonzalez, who is throwing with more of an overhand motion.

"It's not rocket science. It was just a little adjustment, but that's all it took."

The Calumet hurler held Eau Claire scoreless, allowed 2 hits, chalked up 4 strikeouts and yielded 1 walk.

Thus far this season, Gonzalez has worked 32 innings with 22 strikeouts en route to a 2-0 record and a 1.69 ERA.
