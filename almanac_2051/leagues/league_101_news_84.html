Red-Hot Lasanta Cools Off Howards Grove
Sunday, April 17th, 2050
Ricky Lasanta of the Black Creek Cowboys was on top of his game today.

The 26-year-old tantalized the Howards Grove Mighty Mites with crisp curves and changeups, mixed in with just enough fastballs to keep the batters honest and came away with a 7-0 shutout.

"He had the changeup working," Howards Grove manager Jay Ewan said in postgame comments. "You knew it was coming and you still grounded out -- and then he threw it even slower."

The Black Creek hurler had 4 strikeouts, gave up 1 walk and surrendered 6 hits in his complete-game effort.

Lasanta told reporters after the game that "keeping the ball down is the key to being successful in this league."

"If you don't do that, they'll make you pay," he said.
