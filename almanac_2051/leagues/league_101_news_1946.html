Reliever of the Year Award Goes to Dulecha
Wednesday, August 10th, 2050
At the young age of only 24, Muenda Dulecha has been named the Sub League 2 Reliever of the Year Award winner for 2050.

The future appears bright for the Kimberly Raiders relief ace, who captured the award with 1 save in 25 relief appearances, 66 strikeouts in 40.1 innings and a record of 2-0 to go with his 0.22 ERA.

He received 49 first place votes out of a possible 72. Reginaldo Savio of the Ripon Orcas finished second in voting with 8 first place votes, while Kika Naitaku of the West Baraboo Blazers finished third with 15 first place votes.
