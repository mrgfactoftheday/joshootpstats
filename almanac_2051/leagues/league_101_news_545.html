Adam Ferdon Says Farewell to Baseball
Sunday, June 19th, 2050
Adam Ferdon said it was an easy decision to announce that this will be his last year of professional baseball.

"I'm 65 years old," the Lake Geneva Marlins reliever said. "Some of the guys in the clubhouse are young enough to be my son. It's time to go before I start getting white hairs."

The 65-year-old Ferdon has made 67 appearances, crafted a 5-4 record, pitched 89.1 innings and fanned 40. He has 11 saves and a lifetime 2.92 ERA.
