NC Best Newcomer Award Hauled in by Rodriguez
Tuesday, October 18th, 2050
The premier rookie performer in the Northern Counties was Pierce second baseman Luis Rodriguez. He was honored today by league officials as the 2050 Rookie of the Year Award winner.

Rodriguez put up some good numbers, including a .209 batting average and .317 on-base percentage, while racking up 13 home runs, 32 RBIs and 43 runs scored.

He received 72 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Joel Jackson of the Taylor Spiders finished second in voting, while Paul Bartek of the Kewaunee Chargers finished third.
