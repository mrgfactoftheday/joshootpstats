Juneau Win Wildcard Series
Sunday, September 4th, 2050
The Juneau White Sox celebrated wildly after a 8-3 win over the Dodge Fighting Scots in the Wildcard Series. At the center of the celebration was Ed Hammond, who was instrumental in getting his team to the Divisional Round.

"I may get a lot of the credit, but it's a team sport," said the Juneau pitcher. "Getting named MVP is nice, but even if I sucked and we made it to the next round, I would have been happy. That's all that matters at this time of year."

Hammond threw 6 innings, with 6 strikeouts, while allowing 3 hits, 1 walk and 1 run.

Dodge manager Dana Sanders said it was a "tough blow" for his team, which had aspirations of winning the Championship this year.

The Divisional Round matchups and schedule will be announced after the Wildcard Series finishes.
