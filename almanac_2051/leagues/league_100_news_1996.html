Rowdy Players Suspended by League
Sunday, August 21st, 2050
After an intense confrontation, both the Thunder Bears Gerhard Alexander and the Condors Jeff Lykins were handed their punishments today. The league commissioner's office issued suspensions for an on-the-field conflict that occurred at a recent Langlade-Menomonee game. Gerhard Alexander will miss 4 games, while Jeff Lykins was suspended for 6 games.

"Sometimes we go too far and have to pay the consequences," Gerhard Alexander commented later. "Hopefully cooler heads will prevail the next time we play."

On the year Alexander has hit .128 with 4 home runs, 12 RBIs and scored 8 runs.

This year Lykins has a 1-3 record with a 2.36 ERA.
