SC's Best Player for 2050 Named
Friday, October 21st, 2050
Dustin Dalrymple, the Waushara third baseman, certainly made an impression on Southern Counties pitchers this past season. He punished them for 75 hits in 320 at-bats, 18 doubles, no triples, 17 home runs, and 50 RBIs and scored 47 times.

That standout hitting earned him the Southern Counties Most Valuable Player Award for 2050.

He received 54 first place votes out of a possible 72. Tad Cole of the Waukesha Capitals finished second in voting with 8 first place votes, while Diego Garcia of the Milwaukee Seminoles finished third with 1 first place vote.
