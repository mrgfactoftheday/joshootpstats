No Runs, No Hits, No Errors, Nobody Left
Monday, June 6th, 2050
In front of thousands of fans at Beach Bums Ballpark today, Luis Ledesma pitched what will surely be the best game of his career, likely the best of the 2050 WBL Triple A season, and perhaps the best game for years to come. Ledesma was flawless for the Mt. Horeb Swordsmen, throwing a perfect game against the Columbus Beach Bums.

"I knew when I took the mound today that I felt good," Ledesma said to reporters after the game. "I had no idea that I felt that good."

Ledesma struck out 4, allowed no hits, surrendered no walks and allowed no baserunners of any kind en route to the 5-0 Swordsmen victory. He retired 27 batters in a row.
