Ortiz Bashes 5 Base Hits
Sunday, June 19th, 2050
Carlos Ortiz put on a hitting clinic today as the Marquette Peach Kings dumped the Iowa Ravens 8-4 at Ravens Ballpark.

The Marquette center fielder made Iowa defenders earn their pay, lashing Iowa pitching for 5 hits in 5 at-bats in the game. He told BNN reporters after the game that his strategy was "just hit it where they pitched it."

"Too often I try too hard to pull the ball," Ortiz said in his postgame press conference. "Today, I just went with the pitch."

Carlos Ortiz hit an RBI single in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, hit an RBI double in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

This season Ortiz has hit 4 home runs and posted a .315 batting average.
