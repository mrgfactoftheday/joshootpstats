Injury Worsens, Return Delayed For Airhogs Gregory
Monday, May 9th, 2050
Coleman Airhogs reliever Mitch Gregory informed reporters that he will be missing additional time because of a setback in his recovery from a sore back. "It's a bit frustrating, sure," Gregory admitted. "I want to be out on the field. At the same time, I don't want to make things worse and miss even more time because I'm pushing it too hard." Airhogs doctors told BNN Gregory would not return for 6 days.
