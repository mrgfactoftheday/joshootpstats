Rehab Setback For Ryan Dodson
Thursday, September 1st, 2050
The Richland Cannonballers announced today that Ryan Dodson would be missing for longer than expected as the 27-year-old starting pitcher recovers from a torn flexor tendon in his elbow. "Ryan had been working out with the team recently, but felt some discomfort yesterday," the Cannonballers GM explained to the press. "After re-examining the injury, the team's medical staff decided that Dodson would need extra time to heal. Their estimate is 6-7 months more recovery time."
