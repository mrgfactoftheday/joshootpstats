Price Takes Divisional Round in Deciding Game
Friday, September 9th, 2050
Mark one up for the Price Penguins.

In the crucial final game, they put away the Taylor Spiders 1-0 at Penguins Ballpark to win the Divisional Round, 2-1 and advance to the League Semi-Finals.

Although his team came up short, Taylor's Sam Sedita was named the series MVP, with many of the league media feeling the series would have been over much sooner if not for his contributions.

"Sedita did a lot to keep Taylor in it, no doubt about that," said Penguins manager Oliver Partlow.

The League Semi-Finals matchups and schedule will be announced after the Divisional Round finishes.
