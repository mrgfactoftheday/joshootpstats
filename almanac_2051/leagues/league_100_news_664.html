Murillo Takes SC's Best Bat Trophy
Friday, July 1st, 2050
Alex Murillo battered Southern Counties pitchers all June and wound up taking home the Batter of the Month Award.

"I've been around Wisconsin Baseball League long enough to know you really have to work hard day in and day out to be able to put up this kind of consistency," the 30-year-old Murillo told reporters. "I put in a lot of work, even on the days I might not take the field, and it's always nice when everything comes together for me like it did this last month."

In 28 games, Murillo hit .260 with 6 home runs, 13 RBIs and scored 17 runs.

Thus far this year Murillo has hit .226 with 8 home runs and 20 RBIs.
