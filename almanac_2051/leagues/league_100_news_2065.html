Hollingsworth Garners SC Batter of the Month Award
Thursday, September 1st, 2050
Rock right fielder Bill Hollingsworth certainly turned heads in August with some impressive numbers. He batted .303 for the month and was voted the Southern Counties Batter of the Month award.
