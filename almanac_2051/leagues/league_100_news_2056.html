Playoffs Won't Happen for Steelhawks
Tuesday, August 30th, 2050
Nick Lathers didn't hide his feelings when asked by reporters how he felt to know his Waupaca Steelhawks are now officially out of postseason contention.

"It's one of the worst feelings you can have as a baseball player," he said.

This is the club's 23rd playoff miss in a row. They last appeared in the postseason in 2027. In total, the Steelhawks have missed the playoffs 26 times in their 30-year history.
