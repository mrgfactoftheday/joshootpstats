St. Croix Sweeps into the League Semi-Finals
Thursday, September 8th, 2050
The St. Croix Blue Sox have swept the Eau Claire Seafarers and are on to the League Semi-Finals. St. Croix won 6-2 today at Blue Sox Ballpark to clinch the Divisional Round.

"We're happy to have put the dagger in when we had the chance," said St. Croix third baseman Chase Carlisle, who was named MVP for the series. "It could have been a tough series to finish if we let them start winning."

Carlisle won the award with a .429 batting average and a .556 on-base percentage. He had 1 home runs, drove in 4 RBI and scored 1 runs.

The League Semi-Finals matchups and schedule will be announced after the Divisional Round finishes.
