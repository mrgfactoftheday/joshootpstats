Scud Sizzles in No-Hitter
Sunday, June 5th, 2050
Rhys Scud was feeling calm about his no-hitter -- until the last three outs.

"You really become conscious of every pitch at that point," he said. "The adrenaline level kicks up even higher. Everybody in the ballpark knows a no-hitter is on the line. The crowd is on its feet. One mistake and it's all over."

There were no mistakes to be had for Scud on this day at Ballpark of Black Creek. The Viroqua Red Claws right-hander was in complete command, not allowing the Black Creek Cowboys a single base hit in the 5-0 victory.

"Obviously, you never think this will happen," he said to BNN reporters after the game. "I don't know if it's really sunk in yet."

Scud faced 31 batters, had 8 strikeouts and gave up 2 walks in a dominating effort.

"He was on from the get-go," Cowboys manager Nick Grinnell said. "He was hitting his spots and keeping us off balance. It was a great game by him."

This season Scud has claimed 1 save and registered a 1-1 record with a 1.88 ERA. He has 12 relief appearances spanning 28.2 innings and has struck out 30.
