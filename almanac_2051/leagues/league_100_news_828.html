Dodge's Foss SC #1 Batter in July
Monday, August 1st, 2050
Reece Foss made many Dodge Fighting Scots fans happy in July.

That's because the veteran 35-year-old first baseman torched opposing pitchers and has won the Southern Counties Batter of the Month award.

Foss batted .271, hit 6 home runs, drove in 17 runs, scored 13 times and compiled a .436 on-base percentage.

This year Foss is batting .245 with 9 home runs and 27 RBIs. In 43 games he has scored 21 times.
