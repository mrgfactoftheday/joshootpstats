Argonauts Clark Ends Career Because of Injury
Wednesday, January 4th, 2051
The La Crosee newspaper reported today that Argonauts starting pitcher Mike Clark has decided to retire from baseball due to a serious setback while recovering from a torn labrum. "I'm disappointed," Clark told BNN. "My worst fear is a reality and I won't be coming back to the game."

His career stats show that Clark has racked up 3 victories and 3 defeats and has recorded an ERA of 2.64.
