Lopez Hurls Perfect Game
Tuesday, April 5th, 2050
At 37, Jose Lopez has just pitched the best game of his life.

Showing great control and stuff nastier than the city garbage dump, the Prescott Waves hurler made history today with a perfect game against the Somerset Bayhawks.

"I'll never pitch better than this," Lopez said later. "That's a weird thing to know with such certainty, but it's true. All the standout games in my life, at all those different levels, none of them come even close to this."

Lopez led the Waves to the 2-0 victory with 8 strikeouts and 27 consecutive batters retired in a brilliantly pitched game.

He faced 27 Bayhawks and retired all 27, striking out 8 of them. The Waves also played error-free ball behind him.

"Seems like Lady Luck was a Waves fan today," Bayhawks manager Danny Parra said.
