Juneau C Will Miss 4 Games, Suspended by League
Saturday, July 30th, 2050
"Deplorable conduct will not be tolerated in this league, especially if it is directed toward our umpires." That was the gist of the league commissioner's notification letter to the Juneau White Sox organization advising them that catcher Shane Sistek had been suspended for 4 games for his heated confrontation with the home plate umpire in yesterday's game with La Crosee. He became furious over a called third strike.

Sistek on the season is batting .209 with 2 home runs. His totals include 12 RBIs and 11 runs scored. He has played in 52 games.
