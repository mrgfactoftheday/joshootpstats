Islanders Win Tight Sub League 1 Division 2 Race
Friday, July 1st, 2050
The Kewaskum Islanders kicked off what they hope is the first of many celebrations tonight, after having clinched the Sub League 1 Division 2.

Championship success is uncharted waters for the Islanders, as the club has yet to win a World Series.

"We know what it takes, we just have to put ourselves in that position to win it," said reliever Kurt Beckham.

The club's record currently sits at 49-30 and the division win is the 5th in team history.

Team owner The owner of the Kewaskum Islanders said it was "an extremely proud day to be at the head of the Islanders."
