Cashton Captures Sub League 1 Division 4 Crown
Wednesday, June 29th, 2050
Members of the Cashton Cubs say winning the Sub League 1 Division 4 title is special, but ultimately they will only be remembered with a championship season.

"We want to put ourselves in the history books and win the World Series," said shortstop Sami Muugi. "We all believe it can happen, it's just a matter of putting in the hard work and getting the breaks."

It's the 2nd division win for the club and they will be vying for their 1st World Series win.

"To see the team get here, it makes you believe in yourself and all the systems you've put in place," said manager Takao Sakai.
