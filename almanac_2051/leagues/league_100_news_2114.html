Revolution Dominate, Sweep Divisional Round
Thursday, September 8th, 2050
The Wood Revolution outclassed the Marinette Millionaires in all facets of the game throughout the NC Divisional Round.

The League Semi-Finals matchups and schedule will be announced after the Divisional Round finishes.
