Vilas Blanks Prairie Thunder, Aguilar at His Best
Saturday, July 23rd, 2050
Juan Aguilar kept the opposition off the scoreboard and the Vilas Eclipse were all smiles after winning 1-0 against the Marathon Prairie Thunder at Ballpark of Vilas today. The left-handed Aguilar gave up no runs in his start, permitted 4 hits and had 11 strikeouts.

"If there was a stat for 'team shutout,' this would be it," Aguilar said later. "My catcher called a great game, guys delivered at the plate and when I messed something up the guys behind me were where they needed to be."

Thus far this season, Aguilar has tossed 70.1 innings in 7 starts, fanned 81 batters, walked 17 and put up a 0.77 ERA with 5 wins and 1 loss.
