Marinette Narrowly Wins Northern Counties District 8
Thursday, September 1st, 2050
The Marinette Millionaires kicked off what they hope is the first of many celebrations tonight, after having clinched the Northern Counties District 8.

Championship success is uncharted waters for the Millionaires, as the club has yet to win a Championship.

"We know what it takes, we just have to put ourselves in that position to win it," said starting pitcher Chris Bisbing.

The club's record currently sits at 52-47 and the division win is the 4th in team history.

Team owner Josh Kleiber Jr. said it was "an extremely proud day to be at the head of the Millionaires."
