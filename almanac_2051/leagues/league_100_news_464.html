Washburn Trades Leppanen for Wood's Wheat
Friday, June 10th, 2050
According to BNN there were mixed emotions by the players involved in the trade between the Washburn Prospects and the Wood Revolution. As part of the deal, the Prospects swapped 32-year-old SS Andy Leppanen and 20-year-old LHP Jarrett Friedjung to the Revolution getting 27-year-old 3B Rem Wheat in return. The players indicated it was good to get a fresh new start in another city, but it was difficult to leave your friends on the old team.


For the year Leppanen is batting .077 with no home runs, 1 RBIs and no runs scored.

So far this season Wheat is hitting .200 with no home runs.
