Marinette Win Wildcard Series
Monday, September 5th, 2050
Chris Bisbing said he couldn't let his team's season come to an end while he was standing on the mound.

The starting pitcher propelled his Marinette Millionaires to a 3-2 win over the Barron Smokies in the Wildcard Series at Millionaires Stadium and they are now off to the Divisional Round.

"I told myself I needed to step up and that my team was counting on me," Bisbing said. "When you've got all your teammates relying on you, you've got to come through in a big way."

The starting pitcher certainly did come through, as he led the team to victory with 7 innings pitched, with 11 strikeouts, while allowing 5 hits, 2 walks and 2 runs.

The Divisional Round matchups and schedule will be announced after the Wildcard Series finishes.
