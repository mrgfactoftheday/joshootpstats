Hurler Accepts SC Player of the Week Trophy
Monday, July 4th, 2050
Justin Moritz doesn't have the arm of a youngster, but he's still capable of putting together quality stretches of baseball. Last week was one such instance as Moritz cruised to 2 wins and no losses for the Waukesha Capitals and took the Southern Counties Player of the Week Award.

The 31-year-old was at the top of his game last week, eating up 17 innings while posting a 0.00 ERA. He struck out 14 and walked 1.

To date this year, Moritz shows a 6-0 record, 10 starts, 71.2 innings, 61 strikeouts and a 0.75 ERA.
