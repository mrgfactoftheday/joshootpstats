Queen Garners SL1 Top Skipper Award
Friday, August 12th, 2050
The Mt. Horeb Swordsmen had an outstanding season, much of it due to their astute and gifted skipper. For his managerial excellence, Elijah Queen reaped the reward of being voted Sub League 1 Manager of the Year.

"A manager is only as good as the players he's managing," Queen told reporters. "If I didn't have the talent to work with, making all the right moves wouldn't have helped a bit."

Under Queen the Swordsmen finished first in the Sub League 1 Division 3 with a record of 47-33.
