Sanchez Fires No-Hitter
Wednesday, May 25th, 2050
The Pepin Ninjas relied on no-hit stuff from starter Luis Sanchez to defeat the Ashland Aztecs 3-0 at Ninjas Stadium today.

"It's quite a thrill," Sanchez told the media afterward. "This is a top-notch league. A no-hitter against any team in the league is something special. I can't believe I did it."

To get his name logged in the Northern Counties records, he stopped Ashland cold and hitless, surrendering 3 walks and fanning 10 of the 30 batters he faced.

Pepin manager Zach White was in a jovial mode after the game, kidding with reporters that Sanchez "made managing easy today."

"I didn't have hardly any decisions to make like I usually do," he said. "I didn't even have to come up with a pinch hitter."
