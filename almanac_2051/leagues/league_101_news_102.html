Upchurch Spins Masterpiece, No-Hits Berlin
Friday, April 22nd, 2050
Black Creek Cowboys starter Lindsay Upchurch said he was standing behind the mound when the scoreboard caught his eye, confirming what he already knew: He was one out from a no-hitter. For a couple of seconds, he didn't move.

"I took a breath and told myself 'This is the last one,'" Upchurch recalled.

The right-hander collected himself, got the final out, and finished one of the greatest performances of his career.

The Black Creek right-hander no-hit the Berlin River Bandits 1-0 at Ballpark of Black Creek, finishing with 2 walks and 6 strikeouts, facing only 28 batters.

"I knew for a while it was a no-hitter," Upchurch admitted later. "But I really didn't worry about it until the last out. I thought I just wanted to win the game, and then I realized I wanted the no-hitter, too."
