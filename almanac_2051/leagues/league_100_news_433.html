Dane Blanks Wolverines, McGill Wins
Monday, June 6th, 2050
Ryan McGill kept the opposition off the scoreboard and the Dane Maples were all smiles after winning 2-0 against the Winnebago Wolverines at Dane Fields today. The right-handed McGill gave up no runs in his start, permitted 2 hits and had 8 strikeouts.

"If there was a stat for 'team shutout,' this would be it," McGill said later. "My catcher called a great game, guys delivered at the plate and when I messed something up the guys behind me were where they needed to be."

So far this season McGill has a 2-2 record, 5 starts, 36 innings, 24 strikeouts and a 1.75 ERA.
