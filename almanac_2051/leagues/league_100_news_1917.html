Lafayatte's Joe Mathes Suffers Setback
Tuesday, August 2nd, 2050
The Lafayatte Dingos plan to take it slow with closer Joe Mathes, knowing that his recovery from a sprained ankle would likely include a few bumps in the road. A team spokesman told BNN, "Joe has up days and down days. It's still a process. He was feeling really good and then he had a step back. The doctors say it will be about 4-5 weeks before he can be activated."

Mathes has nailed down 8 saves to date. In 15 innings he has fanned 4 batters and walked 10. His pitching record is no wins and 3 losses with a 4.20 ERA.
