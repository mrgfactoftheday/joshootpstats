Diaz Tosses No-Hitter Against Jackson
Friday, July 22nd, 2050
In a dominant outing, Kyle Diaz of the La Crosee Argonauts totally silenced the bats of the Jackson Cowboys, finishing with a no-hitter.

In the Argonauts' 3-0 victory, Diaz faced only 29 batters, surrendered no hits, had 7 strikeouts and 1 walk.

"Honestly, I didn't even know the situation was what it was until the seventh inning," La Crosee manager Travis Beu said. "I happened to look up there to see how many hits we had gotten, and I happened to notice that they hadn't gotten any."

Commenting on the defeat, losing manager Troy Harris told reporters in the clubhouse that he had a "pretty simple" analysis of the game.

"The Argonauts had all the hits and all the runs, so they won," he snapped. "But yeah, Diaz pitched a heck of a ballgame."
