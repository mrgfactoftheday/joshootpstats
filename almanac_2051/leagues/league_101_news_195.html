Waveriders Lose Ammanati for Season
Sunday, May 8th, 2050
The Waterloo Waveriders were dealt a severe blow when Maurizio Ammanati was hurt throwing a pitch recently in a game against the Fredonia Rockets. After the game, the team trainer reported Ammanati might have to spend a lengthy time on the injured list. Today doctors confirmed it, ruling the 25-year-old closer out for the rest of the season with elbow inflammation. They indicated it would take Ammanati at least 3 months to heal.

Ammanati has collected 2 saves for the year with 12 strikeouts and 9 walks in 12 innings while registering a 1.50 ERA and a 0-3 won-lost record.
