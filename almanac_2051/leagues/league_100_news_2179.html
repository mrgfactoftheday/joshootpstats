Hunt Gets Award for Relief Work
Sunday, October 16th, 2050
When Matt Hunt jogged in from the bullpen this season, opposing teams knew they were going to have a tough time putting up runs.

The La Crosee Argonauts starting pitcher turned in a dominant campaign and has been named the Southern Counties Reliever of the Year Award winner for 2050.

His award-winning season saw the bullpen ace get 16 saves in 26 relief appearances, 96 strikeouts in 79 innings and only 15 walks. He had a record of 6 wins and 1 loss to go with his 0.91 ERA.

"I hope I can pitch like this every year," he said.

He received 54 first place votes out of a possible 72. Cody Cobas of the Juneau White Sox finished second in voting with 8 first place votes, while Mario Jaime of the Iowa Ravens finished third with 10 first place votes.
