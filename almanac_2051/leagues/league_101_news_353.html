Bryan Haile Out of Action
Sunday, May 29th, 2050
Mighty Mites starting pitcher Bryan Haile will be spending 7 weeks on the shelf according to his manager. He was hurt in a recent game against Pell Lake throwing a pitch and suffered a hamstring strain. The Howards Grove GM told BNN no decision had been made on his replacement. He said an announcement was forthcoming, probably tomorrow.

So far this season, Haile has logged a 2-1 record with a 1.03 ERA. He has started 6 games, hurled 26.1 innings and registered 20 strikeouts and 9 walks.
