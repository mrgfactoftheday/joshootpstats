Waukesha Ace Takes Pitcher of the Year Award
Thursday, October 20th, 2050
When the name of Justin Moritz appeared on the Waukesha lineup card in 2050, it was a virtual guarantee of a victory or at least a well-pitched game. For his brilliant performance this season he was named the Southern Counties Pitcher of the Year Award winner today.

He started 21 games, running up a 8-5 record and a 1.02 ERA. Moritz held opponents to a .140 average while striking out 123, walking 22 and allowing 72 hits in 149.1 innings of work.

He received 62 first place votes out of a possible 72. Sterling O'Connor of the Crawford Ironmen finished second in voting with 10 first place votes, while Lance Kirchhoff of the Dane Maples finished third.
