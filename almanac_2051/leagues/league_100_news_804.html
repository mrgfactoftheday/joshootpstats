Menomonee's Rich Dyer Injured
Wednesday, July 27th, 2050
Menomonee Condors shortstop Rich Dyer left yesterday's game against the Waupaca Steelhawks after suffering a herniated disc colliding with a player at a base. Dyer was taken to the clubhouse for examination by team doctors. A team spokesman told BNN today Dyer is expected to miss 5 weeks.

This year Dyer has collected a .212 batting average to date with 39 hits in 184 at-bats, 14 RBIs, no home runs and scored 10 times. He has played in 62 games.
