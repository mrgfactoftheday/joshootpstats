Francisco Sizzles in No-Hitter
Sunday, May 29th, 2050
Florence is abuzz in the wake of a masterful no-hitter by Manny Francisco.

The 22-year-old hurler was dominating against the Oconto Invaders, leading the Bandits to a 1-0 win at Invaders Ballpark today.

"I could tell early on that it was going to be a long day for us," Oconto manager Rory Lutz said afterward. "He made us look real bad. He was that good. And still going strong at the end of the game."

Francisco had 3 strikeouts and gave up 5 walks en route to the no-hitter.

"Yeah, this was a special day for me," Francisco said. "I had my good stuff and good movement. Good location, too. Even when I made a mistake, I got away with it today."
