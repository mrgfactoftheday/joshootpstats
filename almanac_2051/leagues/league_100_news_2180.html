Lund Grabs NC Reliever of the Year Award
Sunday, October 16th, 2050
His manager and teammates call Tom Lund a true bullpen ace.

The Northern Counties calls him the winner of the Reliever of the Year Award for 2050.

The Clark Clowns relief specialist out of Eau Claire, Wisconsin, won the award with 2 saves in 25 relief appearances, 95 strikeouts in 74 innings and 12 walks.

He compiled a record of 3 wins and no losses and had a 0.73 ERA.

He received 65 first place votes out of a possible 72. Roger Camargo of the Burnett Spikes finished second in voting with 7 first place votes, while Shane Gier of the Waupaca Steelhawks finished third.
