Thrilling Win Pushes Dane to Next Round
Sunday, September 4th, 2050
Lance Kirchhoff was a picture of composure and ferocity on the mound during the Wildcard Series.

Off the mound, with a 6-2 victory secured and his Dane Maples having just eliminated the Lafayatte Dingos, he couldn't help but smile.

"It was all business when I was on the mound, but now I can relax, knowing we made it through," he said. "I don't remember being as focused on any start in my career as I was tonight."

That focus paid off, as the Dane starter was named MVP as he threw 6.1 innings, with 5 strikeouts, while allowing 1 hit, 1 walk and 1 run.

Lafayatte manager Scott Corona gave Kirchhoff credit for the strong effort and said it's not how his club envisioned the season coming to an end.

"You tip your cap to them and try to learn from it," he said solemnly.

The Divisional Round matchups and schedule will be announced after the Wildcard Series finishes.
