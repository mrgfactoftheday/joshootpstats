Blue Sox Crowned Champions
Wednesday, October 5th, 2050
The St. Croix Blue Sox ignited a huge celebration as they beat the Waushara Sharks 6-0 to win the Championship.

St. Croix won the series 4-3, capturing the title for the 1st time.

"We stepped it up at the right time, and now we're the champs," said St. Croix manager Luis Soto. "It's a great feeling."

The champs finished the regular season 61-39 and claimed second place in the Northern Counties District 7 West.

In the jubilant clubhouse after the decisive game, series MVP and right fielder Danny Hernandez said the Blue Sox have "great fans and they deserve this."

"I am so happy for our city right now," he said.
