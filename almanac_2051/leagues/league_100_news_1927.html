Marentes Holds Whalers Scoreless
Thursday, August 4th, 2050
The Rock Swingers shut out the Racine Whalers 4-0 today behind the strong pitching of Dave Marentes. Marentes surrendered only 5 hits in recording the complete-game victory, and struck out 11.

Following the last out, Marentes appeared to celebrate a little too boisterously for Racine manager Chris Owens, who could be seen scolding the famously outspoken right-hander as the clubs left the field. Marentes's own manager then intervened with some words for his counterpart.

The Whalers skipper would not comment on the exchange, but at the Swingers press conference, Marentes confirmed that it was regarding the celebration.

"He basically told me I was being a lousy winner," Marentes said.

"And I told him he was being a lousy loser," Rock manager Colt King added.
