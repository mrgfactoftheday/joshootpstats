Eager Tosses No-Hitter Against Fighting Scots
Monday, June 13th, 2050
Craig Eager relied on excellent pitch selection and pinpoint control to hold Dodge hitless in a 7-0 win at Ballpark of Dodge today.

"Nobody goes out to pitch a no-hitter," he said later. "We just go out and try to get a win. A 10-9 win is better than a 1-0 loss. My pitching buddies might not be too happy giving up nine runs but a win is a win. The key to it today was I mixed my pitches well. I kept the Fighting Scots off balance and guessing the whole game."

The 36-year-old starter rung up 3 strikeouts and no walks in whipping the Fighting Scots.

"When a guy is throwing strikes the way he was and is locating well, it is going to be a long day for any club," Dodge skipper Dana Sanders said.

In 6 starts this season Eager has crafted a 3-1 record with a 1.30 ERA.
