Door Advances to Divisional Round
Monday, September 5th, 2050
Jake Hill was a picture of composure and ferocity on the mound during the Wildcard Series.

Off the mound, with a 6-2 victory secured and his Door Citadelles having just eliminated the Washburn Prospects, he couldn't help but smile.

"It was all business when I was on the mound, but now I can relax, knowing we made it through," he said. "I don't remember being as focused on any start in my career as I was tonight."

That focus paid off, as the Door starter was named MVP as he threw 6 innings, with 4 strikeouts, while allowing 3 hits, no walks and 2 runs.

Washburn manager Jose Rivera gave Hill credit for the strong effort and said it's not how his club envisioned the season coming to an end.

"You tip your cap to them and try to learn from it," he said solemnly.

The Divisional Round matchups and schedule will be announced after the Wildcard Series finishes.
