Plummer Hurls No-Hitter
Thursday, June 9th, 2050
It was the game that pitchers dream about: the no-hitter.

It happened today at Speed Devils Ballpark. Donny Plummer of the Mauston Centurions was right on target against the Greenwood Speed Devils in a 8-0 victory. Plummer zeroed in on them, holding them hitless with 8 strikeouts and 3 walks.

"I just had it all today," Plummer told BNN reporters after the game. "Very good stuff and great location. That is a combination that's hard to beat."

Over the season Plummer has thrown 48.2 innings and dished out 38 strikeouts with a 1-2 mark and a 2.22 ERA.
