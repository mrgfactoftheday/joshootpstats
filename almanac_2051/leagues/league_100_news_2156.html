Waushara Takes League Finals in Deciding Game
Saturday, September 24th, 2050
The stakes were high at Sharks Ballpark tonight and the Waushara Sharks stepped up when it counted.

Edging the Iowa Ravens 4-3 to wrap up the League Finals, Waushara won the clinching game by a 2-0 score.

"You've got to fight that doubt and that little part of you that says you can't do it or you're going to fail," said Waushara right-hander Marco Flores. "The possibility of failure is always in your mind, but you can't let it bring you down."

The failure of the Ravens to advance was certainly weighing heavily on the team's manager.

"It's a crushing loss and a tough way to lose a series," Kevin Vespucci said. "In the end there can only be one Championship champion and we're just devastated it's not going to be us."

Flores's strong pitching earned him the nod as series MVP. He had a 0.00 ERA over 14 innings pitched. He added 19 strikeouts with a 2-0 record.

The Championship matchups and schedule will be announced after the League Finals finishes.
